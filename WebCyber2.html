Cyber Awareness: Spotting Fake Login Pages

# Beware of Phishing

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers mimic trusted websites like Google, Facebook, or Instagram to steal your login details. These fake pages are often hard to distinguish from the real ones.

Always check the website URL carefully. Ensure it's secure (starts with https://) and doesn't contain unusual characters or misspellings.

# Stay Safe Online

  • Never click on suspicious or unexpected links in emails.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
  • Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Report any page that looks suspicious or asks for credentials unnecessarily.
